Our Story

We're frying up fresh mochi donuts in the traditional pon de ring style! These donuts not only have a light and airy interior, but they also provide a unique pop and chew when bitten into.
Our mochi muffins have a crisp exterior but an incredibly soft and chewy interior. These go perfectly with your morning cup of coffee or as a mid afternoon pick me up snack!
Our mochi muffins come in 3 flavors: brown sugar, matcha, and thai tea. The glazes for our mochi donuts rotate seasonally, but some main stays are our matcha sugar and black tea glazes.
